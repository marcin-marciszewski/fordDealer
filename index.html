The Versa Connect take these attributes and focuses on extending them seamlessly into the wheelchair accessible aspects of our vehicle. It is our aim to produce a conversion that is both practical and stylish, with and attention to detail that provides the high quality look and feel throughout the vehicle

The Versa Connect is available in two wheelbases. The Short Versa Connect can accommodate the wheelchair user and up to 3 seated passengers (including the driver) or up to 5 seated passengers without the wheelchair. The Long Versa Connect can accommodate the wheelchair user and up to 5 seated passengers (including the driver) or up to 7 seated passengers without the wheelchair.

Twin sliding allow for ease of passengers access with a lightweight manual ramp to the rear for wheelchair access. As an option extra, the ramp can have the added ability, using a simple lever, to fold at into the vehicle so that when the wheelchair user in not traveling, a huge and easily accessible boot space is created making the Versa Connect even more practical.
